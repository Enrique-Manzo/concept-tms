A miryad of solutions for your company

FlowApp module components to enhance your current TMS

Modules are a series of features that companies that are not using FlowApp can plug in to their current TMS without using the core FlowApp system.

Connect your TMS to the FlowApp neural machine translation engine and get instant translations that your vendors can later edit and improve to achieve final translation products. This module works as a browser plug-in that tracks the number of translated words while keeping the content private. You can later check the number of words translated by FlowApp, train a proprietary machine translation engine, and download automatically-generated translation memories.

It's not easy to count the number of words or word items in every language. The FlowApp word-count engine supports alphabetic, alphasyllabary, syllabary, and ideogram scripts. You can also configure the word count rules for agglutinative, synthetic, and polysynthetic languages. This module allows you to upload your source files and get a detailed and CAT-tool compatible word count.

Ever wondered who your most efficient translators are? Do you want to know how your per-word income varies month over month? The financial tracker module provides you with a powerful dashboard to explore detailed financial metrics of your translation business. Companies using the financial tracker report a better resource allocation within a month and a 30% increase in profit within six months.
